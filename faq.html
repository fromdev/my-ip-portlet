Frequently Asked Questions About My IP Portlet

This page covers some common questions and concerns related to the My IP Portlet

Why is My IP Portlet Showing my IP as 127.0.0.1 or 0:0:0:0:0:0:0:1 ?

If you are accessing this portlet locally, it may show your address as 127.0.0.1 or 0:0:0:0:0:0:0:1 (in case of IPV6). To check you real IP address seen by external world you need to access this from a remote machine outside the local area network.
